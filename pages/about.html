Welcome To Momenta

“Distract people from their distractions, by momentarily taking them out of autopilot and reminding them of how they really want to spend their time”

The idea of Momenta originates from Project Manager Janac, who struggled to find an application that holds users accountable for wasted time. Most productivity apps were easy to ignore for the everyday procrastinator and would eventually lead to loss of interest. Momenta aims to hold users accountable by sending constant reminders/notifications to the user at a specified time interval. Because these notifications are easy to ignore in most cases, the application will include a key feature that takes over the user’s device and ensures that the user records progress or lack thereof. Momenta also plans to have a reward based system that will give users badges upon completion of certain milestones. The application will also have statistical features that will show users the progress they have made on their tasks, by displaying graphs, charts and progress bars. The goal is to encourage users to keep being productive and making progress in their everyday life.

Momenta is currently in the final stages of development. Most of the functionality has already been implemented. The final stage of the development requires the completion of the gamification features and the full stack testing of the application. The team aims to be done coding the application by the end of October 2016, although testing will continue until all bugs and any other issues are addressed.
